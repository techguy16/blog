Enderman's channel terminated: My opinion

techguy16 November 5, 2025

Note: I wrote this before Enderman's channel was reinstated at around 11:30AM NZDT.

If you've been in the Wintuber community for a while, you've probably heard by now that Enderman's channel has been terminated by YouTube because they "received multiple third-party claims of copyright infringement", along with his other accounts linked to the same Google account.

Yes, I do mean that Enderman, with all their ~385K subscribers.

Currently, YouTube is terminating channels which are linked to an account called 棺のスターレイル遊び with the channel ID UCJTCXGi0ctZ-b4aNjHurulg. The channels were linked through an AdSense account as Russian YouTubers were using it to get around sanctions, and due to Google's totalitarian scheme where "if one channel gets terminated, they all do". What they were doing was a violation of the ToS, but Google could've just demonetized them all.

I think YouTube has gone too far with their AI force-feeding, even allowing it to terminate channels, like in this case. Imagine having Gemini, which is the worst and most innacurate AI chatbot ever, decide. Yes, there have been clear violations of the Terms of Service, but is terminating all the channels required? No. The best solution would've been closing the AdSense account which links the channels, but instead Google decided to terminate them all.
